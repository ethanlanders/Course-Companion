Solution Statement & Characteristics

Solution Statement

This desktop application assists educators in modifying their course material
for increased accessibility and better alignment with student needs. It employs document scanning
to identify areas that may benefit from modifications by offering general suggestions.


We will create a desktop application that will analyze course material, provide
relevant suggestions to increase accessibility for students, and provide a generated output report for educators.

Solution Characteristics

Course Companion performs the following functions:
Analyze course material for relevant keywords
Outputs a results page, giving suggestions and reasons behind said suggestions
Does not alter the curriculum, but offers suggestions for accessibility improvements for the course material
