Problem Statement & Characteristics

Problem Statement

Educators often face challenges in meeting the individual needs of students
on the autism spectrum. They may lack awareness of how course materials could be inaccessible
to some students and struggle to make necessary improvements to enhance accessibility.

Problem Characteristics

Educators sincerely try to meet their students' educational needs, though they may face challenges in addressing every student's needs.
It's essential to acknowledge that educators are actively striving to enhance accessibility.
Educators could benefit from additional support in their efforts.
